About GEORGEL

A Foundation Built on Compassion & Action

GEORGEL Cancer Foundation is a Nigerian civil society organization committed to reducing the burden of cancer through awareness, advocacy, screening, and partnerships with leading health institutions and government agencies across Nigeria.

From free breast and cervical cancer screenings to high-level stakeholder engagements, we are on the ground — in communities, hospitals, media houses, and government offices — making cancer prevention a national priority.

🔬
Early Detection

Free screenings in communities across Jos and North-Central Nigeria

📢
Advocacy

Engaging government, media, and professional bodies for systemic change

🤝
Partnerships

Collaborating with JUTH, NMA, MWAN, NCS, and federal ministries

💊
Medical Support

Donating supplies and supporting cancer warriors on their journey
